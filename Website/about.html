About Us

Empowering the Nation is a local SME founded by Precious Radebe, dedicated to providing essential skills training for domestic workers and gardeners. Inspired by her parents and elderly relatives who never had the chance to upskill or pursue formal education, Precious created this initiative to empower others in similar situations.

Our mission is to equip domestic workers and gardeners with valuable skills that make them more marketable in the workforce. By completing our training programs, they can command higher wages or even start their own businesses. Many employers also seek to upskill their household staff, enabling them to provide more specialized services.

We offer a variety of courses that cater to different needs, and customers can select multiple courses for additional discounts. Whether you're an employer looking to enhance your staff's skills or an individual eager to build a better future, Empowering the Nation is here to help.
